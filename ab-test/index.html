A/B Test Preview

David AI Postcard Campaign -- Utah County Home Services

Test Hypothesis

Version A (locked website preview) creates more curiosity and higher engagement because recipients see a real site built for their business. The "sunk cost" of seeing something already built compels action.

Version B (direct contact) reduces friction by removing the intermediate step. Recipients go straight to claiming their website without needing to see a preview first. May convert faster for businesses that just want results.

VERSION A

Locked Website Preview Flow

Business: Price is Right Landscaping (Saratoga Springs, UT)
Category: Landscaping | Score: 90/100
QR Destination: Locked preview website → contact CTA

The postcard shows a screenshot of the website we already built. The QR code leads to the live site, which is gated halfway down. Recipients must contact us to unlock the full version.
